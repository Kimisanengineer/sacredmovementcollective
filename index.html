My Journey

Born and raised in the Pacific Northwest, my journey with movement began as a child exploring the ancient forests and coastal waters. I come from a lineage of Coast Salish healers and have spent over a decade studying indigenous somatic practices across Turtle Island.

My teaching weaves together traditional earth wisdom with contemporary understanding of trauma-informed practice. I believe that true healing happens when we remember our inherent connection to the land and listen to the intelligence of our bodies.

When I'm not guiding practices, you'll find me foraging for medicinal plants, creating earth pigments for ritual art, or sitting in ceremony with community elders.
